About Us

IMBA is a gaming token created by gamers for gaming enthusiasts. Our goal is to decentralize the gaming industry by offering a new platform for gamers, game developers, streamers and fanatics.

Why IMBA

Our platform will allow gamers to play - no way we’re taking the fun out! Gamers are allowed to convert their gaming equity to Cryptocurrency or NFT. Streamers, content creators, and fans are rewarded with IMBA tokens by simply using the platform. Game Developers can launch their games that can assimilate IMBA tokens into their own game economy for a more decentralized and fair gaming environment.

Vision

Our vision is to converge Gaming and Cryptocurrency that builds a strong network of gamers, game developers, content creators, and fans who can trade, purchase, and gift themselves and have ownership of their digital assets.
