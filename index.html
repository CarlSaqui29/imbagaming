About Us

IMBA is a gaming token built by gamers for gamers. We aim to decentralize the gaming community by providing a new platform for gamers, streamers, game developers and fans. It will allow Gamers to game and be able to convert gaming assets to cryptocurrency or NFT, allow Streamers/content creators/fans to be rewarded in IMBA token by just using our platform, give way to game developers to launch their games and integrate IMBA tokens in their own game economy for a more decentralize and fair gaming environment.

VISION

Our Vision is to connect games and blockchain, allow people to trade virtual items or NFT in our platform, allow for content creators and fans to be rewarded in cryptocurrency, and allow fans to purchase and reward their favorite content creator, and allow gamers to have ownership of their digital assets, and be the medium to introduce blockchain and its potential to the youth through gaming, to give access to everyone to use games and farm through yield farming, and to provide an opportunity for the gamers to yield farm while gaming.

Tokenomics

To reward IMBA holders we will implement a Redistribution fee of 10% in every transaction. 5% of token will go to holders, 2% is burned, 2.5% back to liquidity and 0.5% goes to Dev wallet with 1 year lock up and has a vesting period of 2 years.
